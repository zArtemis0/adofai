Step onto the court and join the high-flying action of "Basket Bros," a fast-paced basketball game where you team up with your bros to dominate the competition. With its retro-inspired graphics and addictive gameplay, "Basket Bros" delivers an arcade-style basketball experience that will keep you hooked for hours on end. Whether you're shooting hoops solo or challenging your friends in multiplayer mode, get ready to show off your skills and become the ultimate baller.

Game Guide

Here's a guide to the controls in "Basket Bros":

Controls

  • WASD + G
  • Arrows + L

Master these controls to dribble, shoot, and dunk your way to victory on the court. With precise movements and teamwork, you can outmaneuver your opponents and score epic baskets. So grab your basketball and get ready to hit the hardwood in "Basket Bros"!
